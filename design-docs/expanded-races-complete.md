## üéØ Design Philosophy

The world of Aethermere is home to **20 playable races** organized into 3 tiers:

- **Core Races (7)** ‚Äî The dominant civilizations, each controlling a major region
- **Common Races (6)** ‚Äî Established peoples with smaller territories or shared lands
- **Exotic Races (7)** ‚Äî Rare, mysterious peoples from the fringes of the known world

Race tier affects starting experience:
- **Core** ‚Äî Full homeland with 5 towns, strong political presence, easy early game
- **Common** ‚Äî Smaller homeland (2-3 towns) or shared territory, moderate start
- **Exotic** ‚Äî 1 settlement or nomadic, rare in the world, harder start but unique abilities

- **Halflings are Neutral-to-Friendly with nearly everyone**: they're traders, not fighters
- **Dragonborn and Genasi respect each other**: elemental kinship
