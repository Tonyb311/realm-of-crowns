## ğŸ  LIVING IN FOREIGN LANDS

A player of any race can live in any town, but there are consequences:

### Racial Majority Bonuses
- If you live in a town where your race is the majority: **+10% all profession bonuses**
- If you live in a town where your race is welcome (Friendly+): **Normal, no bonus**
- If you live in a town where your race is distrusted: **-5% profession yields, NPCs charge more**
- If you live in a town during a Blood Feud: **-15% everything, risk of PvP attacks, can't run for office**

### Racial Minority Opportunities
Being a minority isn't ALL bad:
- A **Dwarf Blacksmith in a Human town** brings skills they don't have locally â€” 
  they can charge premium prices because the competition is weaker
- An **Elf Enchanter in Orc lands** is rare and valuable â€” Orcs need enchanted weapons 
  but can't make them well themselves
- A **Tiefling Alchemist anywhere** is in high demand because Tiefling potions are the best

This creates interesting choices: stay home for bonuses, or move where your 
skills are rare and command higher prices?

5. **Players can change the world** â€” racial relations aren't fixed,
