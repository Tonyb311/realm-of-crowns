## Section 1: Master XP Table (Levels 1-50)

**Formula:** `XP to Next = floor(10 * level^1.15) + 30`
**HP:** Starting = 10 + CON mod + class bonus. Per level: Warrior/Ranger +4, Cleric/Rogue/Bard +3, Mage/Psion +2.
**Stat Points:** 1 per level (50 total over 50 levels, D&D-aligned bounded accuracy)
**Stat Cap:** 20 (modifier +5). Cost curve: 1 pt (11-14), 2 pt (15-17), 3 pt (18-19), 4 pt (20). Max single stat from base 10 costs 20 points.
**Max Level:** 50

## Section 2: Level Milestone Summary

**Level 1** — Starting level. 40 XP to next. 60 HP. 2 stat points. Encounter cap 25% (roads are merciful). Only face Level 1 monsters. Death costs 3 XP / 1 durability.
- Racial abilities unlock: **Versatile Learner** (Human), **Keen Senses** (Elf), **Deepsight** (Dwarf), **Intimidating Presence** (Orc), **Harthfolk Luck** (Harthfolk), **Breath Weapon** (Drakonid), **Hellish Resistance** (Nethkin)

**Level 2** — 52 XP to next. Encounter cap rises to 35%. Monster range expands to 1-2. Death costs 5 XP.

**Level 3** — Professions unlock (PROFESSION_UNLOCK_LEVEL = 3). **First Tier 0 class ability CHOICE** (pick 1 of 3). Death durability jumps to 3. Monster range 1-4.

**Level 4** — No new unlocks. Monster range still 1-4.

**Level 5** — **Second Tier 0 class ability CHOICE** (pick 1 of 3). Death XP penalty rises to 12. Death durability reaches maximum 5. Monster range expands to 2-7 (no more Level 1 monsters).
- Racial abilities unlock: **Diplomatic Tongue** (Human), **Elven Accuracy** (Elf), **Dwarven Resilience** (Dwarf), **Relentless Endurance** (Orc), **Small and Sneaky** (Harthfolk), **Draconic Scales** (Drakonid), **Infernal Sight** (Nethkin)

**Level 6** — HIGH_LEVEL_ENCOUNTER_MULTIPLIER kicks in (1.15x encounter chance). Death XP stays 12.

**Level 7** — Last level with defined monster range (2-7).

**Level 8** — **Third Tier 0 class ability CHOICE** (pick 1 of 3). Death XP penalty rises to 15. Monster range becomes unrestricted ("Full").

**Level 9** — No new unlocks.

**Level 10** — **Specialization selection available.** First spec ability auto-granted (Tier 1). Major milestone.
- Racial abilities unlock: **Rally the People** (Human), **Communion with Nature** (Elf), **Master Forger** (Dwarf), **Blood Fury** (Orc), **Silver Tongue** (Harthfolk), **Elemental Resistance** (Drakonid), **Dark Knowledge** (Nethkin)

**Level 11** — Death XP penalty jumps to 20 (highest tier, permanent from here).

**Level 12** — No new ability unlocks.

**Level 13** — **Warrior gains Extra Attack** (2 attacks per Attack action).

**Level 14** — Second spec ability auto-granted (Tier 2a).

**Level 15** — Racial abilities unlock: **Adaptable Crafter** (Human), **Arcane Affinity** (Elf), **Stonecunning** (Dwarf), **Warbeast Bond** (Orc), **Nimble Fingers** (Harthfolk), **Frightful Presence** (Drakonid), **Whispers of the Damned** (Nethkin)

**Levels 16-17** — No new ability unlocks.

**Level 18** — **3rd saving throw proficiency unlocked** (auto-granted, deterministic per class). See Section 8 for per-class saves.

**Level 19** — No new ability unlocks.

**Level 20** — Third spec ability auto-granted (Tier 2b or Tier 3, varies by spec).

**Levels 21-24** — No new ability unlocks. Long gap.

**Level 25** — Fourth spec ability auto-granted (Tier 3 or Tier 4, varies by spec).
- Racial abilities unlock: **Empire Builder** (Human), **Ageless Knowledge** (Elf), **Clan Loyalty** (Dwarf), **Clan Warhorn** (Orc), **Trade Network** (Harthfolk), **Dragon's Hoard** (Drakonid), **Infernal Rebuke** (Nethkin)

**Levels 26-27** — No new ability unlocks.

**Level 28** — **Ranger gains Extra Attack** (2 attacks per Attack action).

**Level 29** — No new ability unlocks.

**Level 30** — **4th saving throw proficiency unlocked** (auto-granted, deterministic per class). See Section 8.

**Levels 31** — No new ability unlocks.

**Level 32** — Fifth spec ability auto-granted (Tier 4 or Tier 5).

**Level 33** — No new ability unlocks.

**Level 34** — **Warrior gains 3rd attack.** **Cleric gains Extra Attack** (2 attacks per Attack action).

**Levels 35-37** — No new ability unlocks.

**Level 38** — **First feat choice** (pick 1 from 12 universal feats). See Section 9.

**Level 40** — Sixth and final spec ability auto-granted (Tier 5 or Tier 6). Capstone.
- Racial abilities unlock: **Indomitable Will** (Human), **Spirit Walk** (Elf), **Ancestral Fury** (Dwarf), **Orcish Rampage** (Orc), **Feast Master** (Harthfolk), **Ancient Wrath** (Drakonid), **Soul Bargain** (Nethkin)

**Level 41** — No new ability unlocks.

**Level 42** — **Warrior gains 4th attack** (maximum, matches D&D 5e Fighter).

**Levels 43-44** — No new ability unlocks.

**Level 45** — **5th saving throw proficiency unlocked** (auto-granted, deterministic per class). See Section 8. Only CHA or STR remain as the 6th unproficient save.

**Levels 46-47** — No new ability unlocks.

**Level 48** — **Second feat choice** (pick 1 from 12 universal feats). See Section 9.

**Levels 49-50** — No new ability unlocks. Pure stat growth.

## Section 3: Class Ability Progression (All 7 Classes)

All 7 classes share the same ability schedule:
- **Tier 0 (pre-spec choices):** Levels 3, 5, 8 (pick 1 of 3 at each)
- **Spec abilities (auto-granted):** Levels 10, 14, 20, 25, 32, 40

**Exception: Psion** uses tier numbering 1-6 instead of the standard 1-5, with tiers 5 and 6 at levels 32 and 40 respectively. Functionally identical schedule.

## Section 7: Gaps & Concerns

### Dead Zones (levels with no new unlocks for extended stretches)

| Range | Levels | Duration | What happens |
|-------|--------|----------|-------------|
| 15-19 | 5 levels | ~23 days | Nothing between racial L15 and spec ability L20 |
| 21-24 | 4 levels | ~20 days | Nothing between spec L20 and spec L25 |
| 26-31 | **6 levels** | ~40 days | Nothing between racial L25 and spec L32 |
| 33-37 | **5 levels** | ~42 days | Nothing between spec L32 and feat L38 |
| 39 | 1 level | ~12 days | Nothing between feat L38 and capstone L40 |
| 41-44 | 4 levels | ~58 days | Nothing between capstone L40 and save L45 |
| 46-47 | 2 levels | ~26 days | Nothing between save L45 and feat L48 |
| 49-50 | 2 levels | ~27 days | Nothing after feat L48 to cap |

The endgame dead zones have been significantly reduced by feat milestones at 38/48 and save milestone at 45. The longest gap is now 4 levels (41-44).

### Tier 0 Choice Levels vs ability-grants.ts

The tier 0 choice levels (3, 5, 8) are consistent across all 7 class files. All use `choiceGroup` format `{class}_tier0_level{3|5|8}` and `requiresChoice: true`. The `ability-grants.ts` service handles these via the choice API (`POST /api/skills/choose-tier0`), not auto-grant.

### Psion Tier Numbering Discrepancy

Psion specs use tiers 1-6 where other classes use tiers 1-5. Specifically:
- Other classes: T1(10), T2(14), T2(20), T3(25), T4(32), T5(40) — two abilities share tier 2
- Psion: T1(10), T2(14), T3(20), T4(25), T5(32), T6(40) — sequential tiers

This is a **data-only inconsistency** — the level schedule is identical. The tier numbers are cosmetic in the current codebase (ability-grants.ts keys on `levelRequired`, not tier). But it could cause confusion if tier numbers are ever displayed to players or used for filtering.

### Warlord Tier Numbering Anomaly

Warlord spec has a subtle tier issue: `Tactical Advance` is tier 3 at level 20, and `Inspiring Presence` is also tier 3 at level 25. Other specs have two tier 2 abilities at levels 14 and 20, then tier 3 at 25. The Warlord instead has tier 2 at 14, tier 3 at 20, and tier 3 again at 25. Functionally irrelevant since grants key on `levelRequired`, but inconsistent with other specs.

### Death Penalty vs XP Gain Proportionality

| Level | Kill XP (typical) | Death XP Loss | Deaths to Lose 1 Kill |
|------:|------------------:|--------------:|----------------------:|
| 1 | 15 | 3 | 5.0 |
| 5 | 30 | 12 | 2.5 |
| 8 | 40 (L8 monster) | 15 | 2.7 |
| 10 | 50 (L10 monster) | 15 | 3.3 |
| 11 | 55 (L11 monster) | 20 | 2.8 |
| 20 | 100 (L20 monster) | 20 | 5.0 |
| 40 | 200 (L40 monster) | 20 | 10.0 |

Death penalties are well-balanced at low levels. At high levels (20+), the flat 20 XP penalty becomes trivial relative to monster kill XP — dying at level 40 costs only 10% of a single kill. This may be intentional (high-level content is gated by encounter difficulty, not death spirals) but worth noting.

### Encounter Level Range Gap

`ENCOUNTER_LEVEL_RANGE` only defines ranges for levels 1-7. At level 8+, there's no range restriction. The comment says "Full" but the actual behavior depends on how `road-encounter.ts` handles the undefined lookup — it presumably allows any monster in the biome. This should be verified in `road-encounter.ts` if exact behavior matters.

### Stat Budget at Cap (D&D Bounded Accuracy)

At level 50, characters have 50 stat points with a cost curve of 1/2/3/4 per point at brackets 11-14/15-17/18-19/20. Stat hard cap is 20 (modifier +5). Maxing one stat from base 10 costs 20 points (4+6+6+4). Second stat to 18 costs 13 (4+6+3). Typical end-state: 20/18/13/12/11/10 + racial mods (50 pts budget). Max attack bonus = +5 (stat) + +8 (prof) = +13, a reasonable ceiling for 50 levels vs D&D 5e's +11 for 20 levels.

### Gold Death Penalty

The 5% gold loss on death (`DEATH_PENALTY.GOLD_LOSS_PERCENT`) does NOT scale with level — it's always 5%. At high levels with large gold reserves, this could be the most painful penalty, which may be intentional as an economic sink.

## Section 8: Saving Throw Proficiencies

D&D-style saving throw system. Each class starts proficient in 2 of 6 saves. Non-proficient saves use ONLY the stat modifier (no proficiency bonus). At milestone levels 18, 30, 45, characters auto-gain additional save proficiencies in a deterministic class-based order.

**Impact:** At level 40+ (proficiency +8), a non-proficient save is 8 points lower than a proficient one. This makes CC and debuff abilities meaningful against specific saves.

**Monsters:** Always add proficiency to ALL saves (unchanged legacy behavior).

**Source:** `CLASS_SAVE_PROFICIENCIES` and `CLASS_MILESTONE_SAVE_ORDER` in `shared/src/data/combat-constants.ts`.

### Save Proficiency Progression by Class

| Class | Base (L1) | +L18 | +L30 | +L45 | Unproficient |
|-------|-----------|------|------|------|-------------|
| Warrior | STR, CON | WIS | DEX | CHA | INT |
| Rogue | DEX, INT | WIS | CON | CHA | STR |
| Ranger | STR, DEX | WIS | CON | CHA | INT |
| Mage | INT, WIS | CON | DEX | CHA | STR |
| Cleric | WIS, CHA | CON | STR | DEX | INT |
| Bard | DEX, CHA | WIS | CON | STR | INT |
| Psion | INT, WIS | CON | DEX | CHA | STR |

**Design notes:**
- All martial classes (Warrior, Rogue, Ranger) gain WIS save at 18 — D&D tradition of "mental defense for fighters"
- All caster classes (Mage, Cleric, Psion) gain CON save at 18 — concentration defense
- Every class ends with 5/6 saves proficient at level 45, leaving exactly 1 vulnerable save
- Bard is the most balanced save profile (DEX, CHA, WIS, CON, STR) — only INT remains unproficient

## Section 9: Feats

Universal feat system unlocking at levels 38 and 48 (2 feats maximum). Player chooses 1 feat per milestone from a pool of 12. Choice is permanent.

**Source:** `shared/src/data/feats.ts`
**API:** `POST /api/characters/choose-feat`, `GET /api/characters/pending-feat`

### Feat List

| Feat | Category | Effect |
|------|----------|--------|
| Precise Strikes | Combat | +1 to all attack rolls |
| Brutal Critical | Combat | Critical hits deal +50% bonus damage |
| Combat Reflexes | Combat | +3 to initiative |
| Tough | Defense | +2 HP per character level (retroactive) |
| Resilient | Defense | Gain proficiency in one additional saving throw (chosen on selection) |
| Iron Will | Defense | +1 to ALL saving throws |
| Natural Armor | Defense | +1 AC permanently |
| Lucky | Utility | Once per combat, reroll any d20 roll |
| Undying | Defense | Death XP and gold penalties reduced by 50% |
| Quick Learner | Utility | +10% XP from all sources |
| Master Artisan | Crafting | +3 to all profession quality rolls |
| Merchant Prince | Crafting | Buy 10% cheaper, sell 10% higher |
| Fortune Favored | Crafting | +15% gold from all sources |

### Feat Milestone Levels

| Level | Milestone |
|------:|-----------|
| 38 | First feat choice (pendingFeatChoice flag set) |
| 48 | Second feat choice |

### Design Notes
- Combat feats (Precise Strikes, Brutal Critical, Combat Reflexes) are class-unrestricted
- Resilient's save choice cannot duplicate an existing proficiency
- Tough grants retroactive HP: at level 38 = +76 HP, at level 48 = +96 HP
- Lucky reroll is once per combat (not yet implemented in combat engine)
- Economy feats (Master Artisan, Merchant Prince, Fortune Favored) won't affect combat but are strong for endgame players focused on crafting/trading
